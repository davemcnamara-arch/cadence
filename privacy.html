Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2025

Introduction

Cadence ("we", "our", or "us") is a music skill progression tracker designed for educational use. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you use Cadence, we collect:

  • Account information: Your name and email address from Google Sign-In
  • Usage data: Songs you add, your learning progress, and skill assessments

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Cadence service
  • Track your music learning progress
  • Allow teachers to view student progress (in classroom settings)

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With your teacher (if you are part of a class)
  • When required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your learning progress data

Children's Privacy

Cadence is designed for educational use and may be used by students under 13 with parental or school consent. We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the service.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact your school administrator or teacher.
