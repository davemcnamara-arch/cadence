Song Library

A living library, built by your students.

Students suggest songs they want to play. Teachers approve, grade by level, and attach YouTube tutorials and chord sheets. And because every approved song — along with all its resources — is shared across every school on Cadence, your library starts full and gets richer over time. Less time building from scratch, more repertoire to choose from on day one.
