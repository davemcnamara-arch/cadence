# Cadence — Teacher Promotional Video Script

**Target audience:** Music teachers, heads of department, school administrators
**Tone:** Confident, inspiring, professional — problem/solution framing
**Format:** Narrated screen showcase with motion graphics or B-roll cuts
**Estimated length:** ~2–3 minutes

---

## [HOOK]

**[SCREEN: Split view — chaotic notebook vs. clean Cadence dashboard]**

> "How do you actually know if your students are making progress? Not just the ones who practise loudly — all of them."

> "Cadence gives you a clear, real-time picture of every student's musical development, so you can spend less time chasing data and more time teaching."

---

## [WHAT IS CADENCE?]

**[SCREEN: Cadence logo animates in — 'Track Your Musical Journey']**

> "Cadence is a music skill progression tracker built for classrooms. It lets students log and self-assess their learning across five instruments — Guitar, Bass, Piano, Drums, and Vocals — while giving you the oversight you need to guide every student individually."

---

## [FEATURE 1: The Pathway System]

**[SCREEN: Student Pathway — 5-level visual map with branching at Level 4]**

> "Every student follows a structured **five-level pathway** for each instrument they're learning. The first three levels build core skills linearly. At Level 4, students choose a specialisation branch — rhythm, lead, fingerstyle — tailoring their journey to their goals."

> "It's a curriculum you can point to. Students know exactly where they are and what they're working towards."

---

## [FEATURE 2: Song-Based Assessment]

**[SCREEN: Grade a Song wizard — checklist, level suggestion]**

> "Students don't just practise songs — they **grade them**. A built-in assessment wizard walks them through a skill checklist tied to their current level, then suggests an appropriate difficulty rating for the song."

> "This turns every song into a formative assessment. Students develop self-awareness. You get documented evidence of their progress."

**[SCREEN: Song Library with ratings, YouTube/Spotify links, chord links]**

> "All graded songs feed into a shared **Song Library** — a growing, student-built resource with links to tutorials, chord charts, and recordings. The more students use it, the more valuable it becomes."

---

## [FEATURE 3: Class Management]

**[SCREEN: Teacher's Classes view — multiple class cards]**

> "Setting up a class takes seconds. Generate a unique **class code**, share it with your students, and they're enrolled. You can run multiple classes, organised by year level or group."

**[SCREEN: Roster view — student names and their instruments]**

> "Your **class roster** gives you an at-a-glance view of who's in the class and which instruments they're tracking."

---

## [FEATURE 4: Progress Heatmap]

**[SCREEN: Heatmap — colour-coded grid, students × instruments, levels shown]**

> "The **Progress Heatmap** is where the picture really comes together. Every student, every instrument, every level — colour-coded in a single view."

> "You can see in an instant who's progressing steadily, who might need a push, and who's ready for the next challenge. No spreadsheets. No manual tracking."

---

## [FEATURE 5: Activity & Submissions]

**[SCREEN: Student Songs / timeline view — recent submissions from students]**

> "As students submit work, it appears in your **activity feed**. You can review every song assessment, see the skill checklist they filled in, and — where needed — adjust the level rating yourself."

**[SCREEN: Flagged Ratings panel]**

> "Cadence even flags songs where student ratings differ significantly from each other — a built-in quality control check that helps you spot where students might need guidance on self-assessment."

---

## [FEATURE 6: Student Detail & Export]

**[SCREEN: Student detail modal — instruments, levels, mastered songs list]**

> "Click any student to see their full profile — every instrument they're tracking, every song they've mastered, and their current level across the board."

**[SCREEN: Export to CSV — class data download]**

> "When report time comes, export your entire class's progress to **CSV** in one click. It's ready to drop straight into your gradebook or LMS."

---

## [FEATURE 7: Student-Driven, Teacher-Informed]

**[SCREEN: Student using Pathway on one side, teacher heatmap on the other]**

> "Cadence is designed around a simple idea: students who can see their own progress are more motivated to make it. They own their journey. You have the oversight to guide it."

> "It's not just a tracker. It's a shared language between you and your students about what musical growth looks like."

---

## [OUTRO / CALL TO ACTION]

**[SCREEN: Cadence logo, tagline: 'Track Your Musical Journey']**

> "Cadence. Clear progress. Better conversations. More time for music."

> "Sign in with your Google account to get started — and have your first class running in under five minutes."
